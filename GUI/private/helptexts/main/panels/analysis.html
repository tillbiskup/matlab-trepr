Data analysis

This panel gives access to different means of data analysis.

The controls

A short description of all the controls of the panel.

MW frequency drift

This can be only used with datasets where for each time trace a microwave frequency value is recorded (as in case of the Freiburg data).

Pressing the Show button will open an additional GUI window displaying the MW frequency versus field. Such, drifts of the MW frequency that have some impact on the signal can easily be revealed.

Net polarisation

Sometimes it is useful to get an idea of the net polarisation of a dataset. Therefore, a sum projection over each time trace is displayed in an additional GUI window upon pressing the Show button.

BG positions

For recording schemes as those used in Freiburg where the background signal is recorded repeatedly over the time course of the measurement at off-resonant position, sometimes it is useful to see where such background traces have been recorded to get an idea whether some "wiggles" are real features of your spectra or artifacts introduced by drifts followed by background subtraction.

Displaying background positions works only in 2D mode and in 1D mode showing spectra (with magnetic field axis).
